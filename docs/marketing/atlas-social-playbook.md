# Atlas Social Marketing Playbook

## Positioning
- Atlas is not positioned as a generic fulfillment provider.
- Core story: Turkish brands selling in local marketplaces can enter the US market with a more controlled operating model, broader demand access, and better channel economics.
- Forbidden shortcut claim: never say "US commissions are always lower." Use category-based, channel-based, and unit-economics framing instead.

## Core Message Pillars
- Channel economics: Amazon, Walmart, Shopify, and mixed-channel route design.
- Operational confidence: LLC, EIN, customs, warehouse, and fulfillment workflow.
- Visible control: dashboard, workflow, documents, AI copilot, and operational signals.
- Growth support: marketplace, advertising, social media, and reporting modules.

## Monthly Output Standard
- Instagram: 12 reels, 8 carousels, daily story batches.
- YouTube Shorts: 8-12 repurposed winners.
- LinkedIn: 12 posts.
- TikTok: 8 test videos.
- Webinar: 2 sessions per month.

## Content Skeleton
- 0-2 sec: hard hook.
- 2-6 sec: pain or friction.
- 6-18 sec: Atlas solution surface.
- 18-28 sec: proof or workflow.
- 28-35 sec: demo CTA.

## Faceless Creative Rules
- Default visual language: Atlas UI, dark surfaces, navy-teal palette, large captions, motion typography.
- Use real product screens before stock footage.
- Warehouse or logistics visuals should support, not dominate, the story.
- Every short-form video must work with captions on and sound off.

## Proof Rules
- Use real product UI, workflow views, and operational checklists.
- Do not use placeholder customer counts, satisfaction scores, or testimonials unless they are verified from CRM or reporting exports.
- Any tax, customs, or fee statement must be reviewed by operations before publishing.

## Funnel Rules
- Primary CTA: request a demo.
- Mid-funnel CTA: profitability analysis or webinar registration.
- All public marketing forms must preserve UTM and entry-point data.
- Admin lead review should include entry-point and source badges.

## UTM Naming Convention
- `utm_source`: instagram, youtube, linkedin, tiktok, meta, partner
- `utm_medium`: organic_social, paid_social, webinar, retargeting
- `utm_campaign`: `yyyy-mm_offer_angle`
- `utm_content`: hook or creative family
- Example: `utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=organic_social&utm_campaign=2026-03_demo_channel-economics&utm_content=reel_margin-hook-a`

## Weekly Review Rhythm
- Weekly: top hooks, saves, shares, profile CTR, demo starts.
- Bi-weekly: winning angles and losing claims.
- Monthly: channel to demo conversion and qualified lead quality.
